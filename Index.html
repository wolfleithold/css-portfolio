About Me

I have always had an affinity for the digital world. When I was 6, my older sister Martine introduced me to the Sega Genesis and the Nintendo 64. I used to watch her play Sonic and Zelda for hours, and was fascinated how all of the data was stored on those small game cartridges. When I got older, I learned what coding was and wanted to learn more about it, but it was so overwhelming to try on my own that I gave up many times. Thankfully I found this bootcamp to teach me things at a healthy pace.
